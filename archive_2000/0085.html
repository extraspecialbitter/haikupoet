I wrote these on the train this morning, deliberately stretching the
bounds between juxtaposition and "soft disconnect" while still trying to
capture a haiku moment. Comments, critiques and catapults appreciated...

        empty golf course --
        my children's sand castles
        carefully preserved

        news from my home town --
        the hawk seems so peaceful
        when circling above
