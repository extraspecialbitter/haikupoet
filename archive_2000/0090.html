        Bryant Park bench
        surrounded by daffodils
        an old man sleeps

        Brooklyn Heights promenade
        a young bulldog
        strains against the leash

        from curb to crosswalk
        and back again
        pigeons gather breadcrumbs

        McSorley's Old Ale House
        generations of sawdust
        cling to our shoes

        leaving Manhattan
        our train
        bathed in sunset
