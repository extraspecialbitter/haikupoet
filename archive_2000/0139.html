        airport delay
        flocks of seagulls
        land and take off

        morning in Montreal
        a joyful reunion
        with our luggage

        Phillips Square
        a pigeon sits on the head
        of a mighty king

        St. Catherine's Street
        a desheveled man begs
        in two languages

        Latin Quarter cafe
        one more hour
        to drink it all in
