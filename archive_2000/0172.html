Two fatal accidents occurred within a day of one another last week at
the same underpass in Natick, MA - just a few blocks from our house.
Over the weekend numerous makeshift memorials of flowers, letters and
signs appeared at the crash site.

        Mass Pike underpass
        a tangled wreckage
        of flowers and words
