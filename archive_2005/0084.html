April chill --
Wakefield's knuckleball
unhittable

April chill --
she doesn't acknowledge
my smile

April chill --
I leave another
voicemail (message)

April chill --
a stranger lights a cigarette
in the crowd

April chill --
a cigarette smoulders
in the fresh mulch

April chill --
the 7:09 too full
for a card game

April chill --
their May-December romance
(still) a month away
