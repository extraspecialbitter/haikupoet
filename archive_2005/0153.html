waiting for the interview --
a gull floats
above the whitecaps

waiting for the interview --
an overflowing
trash can

waiting for the interview --
the receptionist's
R-rated phone call

waiting for the interview --
another candidate
as nervous as me

waiting for the interview --
no time to finish
this haiku
