Veteran's Day --
another letter addressed
to Parris Island

Veteran's Day --
the dog barks
at a falling leaf

Veteran's Day --
a red strobe light
in her bedroom window

Veteran's Day --
a fly trapped
behind the storm window

Veteran's Day --
a bird's nest
in the tallest tree 

--
