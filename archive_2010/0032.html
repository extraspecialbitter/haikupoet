cold April rain --
and it's only
Tuesday

cold April rain --
the dog sniffs
everything

cold April rain --
why do I
live here?

cold April rain --
I no longer remember
your name

cold April rain --
my daughter's birthday
on another continent

cold April rain --
the sediment
at the bottom
of a wine glass
