between skyscrapers

haiku by Paul David Mena
photos by Mary Melodee Mena

between skyscrapers
bryant park bench
chinatown morning
clinging
commuter crossing
finally off-duty
harvard square serenade
listening to the news
Mass Pike traffic
Mass Pike underpass
the NASDAQ falls
a noisy flock
outside starbuck's
south station twilight
unable to escape
wild ride on wall street
